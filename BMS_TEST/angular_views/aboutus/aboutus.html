We felt the necessity of quality food with quality time & quality life for the Mumbaikar who really wants hassle free shopping, but can not compromise with quality see food . Our Malwani marinated fresh fish not only is convenient in cooking but saves time & money to a great extent ,without using any chemicals to preserve .
